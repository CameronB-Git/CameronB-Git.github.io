Exploring My Work

2023

Invoice Dashboard --> During my time at Ron Dearing UTC, I took part

in a week-long Work Experience opportunity at The 55 Group, a small Web

Development/Procurement business based in Hull. In this period, we were

exposed to JavaScript frameworks such as Node.js and Vue.js, enabling us to

create a fully functional Invoice Dashboard. This was a great experience for

me, as it allowed me to gain a better understanding of different programming

languages, as well as giving me a chance to work with new technologies.



Cookie Clicker --> Within my Mobile App Development subject, I worked

on a project to create a Cookie Clicker game. This was a great opportunity

for me to learn how to use Android Studio to create a fully functional

application. Participating in the creation of a cookie clicker game was a valuable

experience that enhanced my comprehension of diverse programming languages

and concepts.



Game Development Video Essay --> Within my Game Development subject, we

were tasked with creating a video essay on a Social and Technical Trend within

the Gaming Industry. I chose to talk about Streaming and Esports for the Social

Trend and Cross-Platform play for the Technical; we then had to discuss the impacts

of these trends, and come to a conclusion on how these trends have affected the

Players, Developers, and the gaming industry as a whole.
